# Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

SUSHITAP does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline and does not require an account, sign up, or internet connection.

## Data storage

Your language, theme, favorite sushi type, current session, and session history are saved only on your own device, using local on device storage. This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

## Third parties

SUSHITAP does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services of any kind, and does not share data with any third party, because no data is collected in the first place.

## Children's privacy

Since SUSHITAP does not collect any personal data, it can be safely used by people of any age.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted in this file with a new effective date.
